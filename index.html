Frequently Asked Questions

Can you guarantee my score will go up?

No. No legitimate credit repair company can guarantee a specific outcome. What we can guarantee is that we will follow the law, work hard on your behalf, and keep you informed.

How long does credit repair take?

Some clients see changes within 30–60 days, but more complex files can take several months. Your timeline depends on the number and type of items being disputed and how quickly bureaus respond.

Do you send the same letters for everyone?

No. We do use templates for efficiency, but the content is customized to your specific accounts, history, and goals.

Will you educate me too?

Absolutely. We want you to understand how credit works so you can protect your progress long after our work is done.
