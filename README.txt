Stayton Credit Solutions - Elite Credit Repair Website
=====================================================

Files:
- index.html  (main one-page site)
- style.css   (all styling for layout, colors, and responsiveness)
- script.js   (score slider + auto year in footer)
- /assets     (place for your logos, PDFs, etc.)

How to use:
1. Upload index.html, style.css, script.js, and the assets folder to your GitHub repo.
2. Turn on GitHub Pages and point it to the root of that repo.
3. Replace placeholder email/phone in the contact section with your real info.
4. Connect the contact form to your email or CRM if you want it live.
